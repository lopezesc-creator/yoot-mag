October

Daniela Lopez Escalante

October 7, 2025

Once a month my body will feel
bigger than my being so I’ll sit
inside myself like a little naked cat
licking uncontrollably to rid myself
of the weirdness

My boobs feel bigger today, they
fill my bra and push out a little
which looks nice in the mirror and
it perks me up a bit
but they manifest an uneasy sore
and I wish you’d hold my naked
body on my bed

Kiss me and soothe your hands on
me but let's wait till I’m wriggling
around for you
to nestle into our collective
warmth.
